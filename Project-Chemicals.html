Chemical analysis software

A big producer of the hardware for laboratories decided to use their experiense and build a common software platform which could be additionally offered to their customers as a paid software.

As a lead designer for the project I was posed with a task to come up with a solution which whould allow us to not only make a modern and clean platform but also template different parts of it so in the future teams could connect additional equipment without the support of the main platform team. The main things to realise that approach were: create a design system, use simple modular layouts for pages and research all the existing software to get the full picture of the possible needs.
