Making emission tracking easier

Problem

A large international, multi-industry corporation recognized the need for a robust internal tool to track production emissions. Driven by new regulations, they also identified a potential business opportunity and decided to develop a POC to pitch the tool to the clients and determine if the tool would be a valuable portfolio addition.

Solution

Within a tight six-week timeframe, our team of three developed a demonstration website covering the tool's core functionality, along with mockups showcasing potential advanced features. We prioritized ease of use, recognizing that the tool would be used intermittently by various individuals, necessitating a clear and intuitive interface for first-time users.

Results

The tool received internal approval and generated significant client interest, leading to ongoing development of a production-level version.

Learnings

  • The project's success stemmed from efficient communication within a lean team and a thorough preparation phase.
  • A strict deadline, coupled with a feature list tailored to the available timeframe, facilitated focused and productive work and stakeholder discussions.
