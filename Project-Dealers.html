CMS for auto dealers

Problem

A prominent European automotive business determined that their current CMS lacked essential features, resulting in a loss of market share among dealers. The system's outdated technology and poor UX mandated a complete redesign.

Solution

Given that the system was to be rebuilt from the ground up, the decision was made to develop an MVP of a new common platform, intended to eventually host the majority, if not all, of the company's offerings. The project also presented an opportunity to bring in the design system from the USA branch. I collaborated with their team and marketing to develop the European theme, which we would begin implementing across other products.

Results

In the initial months following the platform's launch, we received positive feedback from existing customers. We also compiled a backlog of features for future releases. The platform's initial success prompted the business to initiate the migration of another product to the common platform.
